Detection Rules for Threat Actor Simulation & Detection Toolkit

1. Reconnaissance:
   - Monitor for abnormal WHOIS queries and subdomain scans.

2. Weaponization & Delivery:
   - Identify payload creation attempts and suspicious phishing email characteristics.

3. Exploitation:
   - Alert on exploitation attempts targeting known vulnerabilities.

4. Installation:
   - Detect modifications to system startup or registry configurations.

5. Command & Control (C2):
   - Monitor for irregular DNS tunneling and beaconing activities.

6. Exfiltration & Actions on Objectives:
   - Watch for unusual encrypted data transfers or large data exfiltration events.
